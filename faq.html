What is BREW LAB?

BREW LAB is your brewing partner – we provide simple home beer brewing products with a dedicated space to learn the beer brewing craft to ensure a fun, seamless beer brewing experience.

Ordering Products

How do I order?

You can order our products by adding items to your cart and checking-out.

  1. On the header menu, click on The Shop.
  2. We offer complete beginner Bundles as well as Individual Kits to match your brewing needs. Choose your items and the quantity for each item.
  3. Click Next and go through the pages. Your selections will be consolidated at the end of the form.
  4. Once you’ve selected all the items you want to purchase, navigate to the end of the form.
  5. Fill out your personal information, contact details, and delivery address. Click Next.
  6. Read our terms and conditions, tick the box if you agree, and then click Submit.
  7. You’ll receive an email with your order confirmation, delivery details, and payment details.
  8. BREW LAB currently accepts bank transfers. Our bank details will be sent in the order confirmation email. Transfer the correct amount and reply back with a proof of payment.
  9. We’ll confirm your order and set your delivery date.
  10. Receive your order on the delivery date and start brewing!
