FAQs

Have a question? Check our FAQs below.

What is BREW LAB?

BREW LAB is your brewing partner – we provide simple home beer brewing products with a dedicated space to learn the beer brewing craft to ensure a fun, seamless beer brewing experience.

Ordering Products

How do I order?

You can order our products by adding items to your cart and checking-out.

  1. On the header menu, click on The Shop.
  2. We offer complete beginner Bundles as well as Individual Kits to match your brewing needs. Choose your items and the quantity for each item.
  3. Click Next and go through the pages. Your selections will be consolidated at the end of the form.
  4. Once you’ve selected all the items you want to purchase, navigate to the end of the form.
  5. Fill out your personal information, contact details, and delivery address. Click Next.
  6. Read our terms and conditions, tick the box if you agree, and then click Submit.
  7. You’ll receive an email with your order confirmation, delivery details, and payment details.
  8. BREW LAB currently accepts bank transfers. Our bank details will be sent in the order confirmation email. Transfer the correct amount and reply back with a proof of payment.
  9. We’ll confirm your order and set your delivery date.
  10. Receive your order on the delivery date and start brewing!

Can I order in bulk?

Definitely. Email us at purchasing@brewlab.ph for inquiries.

How do I pay for my order?

We currently accept online bank transfer as payment methods. Our bank details will be sent to you via email once you complete your order.

Paying via bank transfer:
  • Order your items normally through any of the two ways described above.
  • Upon verification, our team will provide you with the account details.
  • Transfer your payment to the account given
  • Send a picture of your proof of payment to hello@brewlab.ph.
  • Receive your order without any cashout.

How would I know if my order went through?

You will receive an order confirmation email from BREW LAB. When your order is being processed, our team will email you to verify your order. If you do not receive a confirmation email or text from BREW LAB within 48 hours, contact us at orders@brewlab.ph.

How long does it take to process my order?

When your order is being processed, our team will verify your orders via email. We process orders on weekdays. Orders made during the weekend or in the evenings are processed on the next working day. Once processed, you will receive a confirmation email from BREW LAB.

Can I modify my order?

To prevent miscommunications and minimize inaccuracies, orders are considered final upon checkout. For any revisions, please cancel your order by replying “CANCEL ORDER” to the verification email our team will send you and place your corrected order on brewlab.ph.

How do I cancel my order?

You may only cancel your order if it has not yet been shipped. Contact us at orders@brewlab.ph to confirm the status of your order, and our team will be in touch. If your order can be cancelled, you will receive an order cancellation notification.

Can I order for someone else?

Definitely. If you want your products to be delivered straight to the person you’re ordering for, input his/her delivery address.

Is there a minimum order?

There's no minimum order for delivery. However, shipping will be shouldered by the buyer.

Delivery

What are the areas covered for delivery? Is there a shipping fee?

We currently deliver nationwide. The buyer shoulders the shipping fee.

Can I pick-up my order from the BREW LAB office?

Our operations are streamlined to give you a hassle-free delivery experience. Please use our delivery service for your convenience.

When will I receive my order?

Once you complete your order, the BREW LAB team will be in touch to confirm your order and provide you with the delivery details. Your item will be shipped within one (1) week from order confirmation date. For any special requests, please contact care@brewlab.ph and we’ll see what we can do.

What if I'm not at home when the order arrives?

Your representative may present a copy of your ID.

Brewing Beer

What is beer?

What is life? Beer is life. Jokes aside, beer is an alcoholic beverage for your enjoyment. It consists of water, malt, hops, and yeast.

Wait, I can brew beer at home?

Yes, you can. Don’t worry, we were surprised too. Check out the Beginner’s Page at The Lab to see our guide for brewing beer.

Is it safe to brew beer at home?

Definitely! As long as you follow the proper sanitization steps, home brewing beer is very safe. Check out The Lab to see our guide to home brewing beer.

What if I don’t like beer?

That’s fair, we all have different tastes. That being said, beer does come in different shapes and sizes, flavors and profiles, so we hope you find one that suits your taste palate. We’d be happy to help you find that beer. Hit us up. ;)

I’d like to learn more about beer/home beer brewing.

We’d love to share our knowledge with you. Check out The Lab to see our guide to beer and home brewing beer. We’ll constantly be adding educational materials to ensure that your learning journey doesn’t stop!

Returns

What is your return policy?

All products sold on BREW LAB are subject to quality checks by our purchasing, storage, assembly, and delivery teams. Please inspect your order upon delivery. In the unlikely event that your items are damaged upon delivery, contact us at care@brewlab.ph and we’ll get it sorted out for you.
On a case-to-case basis, we can process requests to return the items within seven days upon delivery. Please make sure that you keep your delivery receipt and the items you want to return.

What products will you accept for returns?

We will replace damaged or wrongly delivered products.

Can I return an item after seven days?

Any problems encountered seven days after the delivery may not be accommodated under our Return Policy. We highly encourage you to review and inspect your medicines upon delivery so that our courier could immediately facilitate the return, exchange, or refund request.

How long is the processing time for returns?

Return orders, if valid, are processed within two to three business days.

What information do you share?

Only our team processing your orders have access to your shared information to ensure your orders are properly delivered. We may use anonymized data for analytics to improve our services or collaborate with partners.
